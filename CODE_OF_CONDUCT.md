# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want dotden to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, public or private.
- Trolling, deliberate intimidation, or personal/political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying these standards and may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate them.

Reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially and in good faith.
